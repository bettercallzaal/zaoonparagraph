what this repo is

This is the working repo behind the newsletter - not the newsletter itself. Drafts get written here, checked against the voice, and pushed to Paragraph as drafts (never auto-published). Everything below is folder, not feature:

drafts/ - in progress, not yet published
published/ - archive of what actually went out
templates/ - daily-3 / deep-dive / recap formats
automation/ - the Paragraph API pipe, draft-only, hard-gated
research/ - strategy and platform notes

the daily-3 builder

Issues get composed and voice-scored at zabalnewsletterbuilder.vercel.app before landing here as a draft.

the voice

all lowercase. no commas. every issue opens with "zm." no emojis, no em dashes, no hype. one signoff: BetterCallZaal on behalf of the ZABAL Team.
