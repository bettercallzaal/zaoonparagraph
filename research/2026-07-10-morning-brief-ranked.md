## Ranked by effort vs. impact

### 1. ZAO Fund deep-dive - do this first

**Effort: low.** The numbers already exist and are public within ZAO - no new mechanism, no cross-team coordination, no new publication. Just write it using `templates/deep-dive.md`, already built.

**Impact: high.** ~$192k in real artifact sales across 32 grantees this season, and the fund's own internal docs say plainly that zero public storytelling exists for any of it. This is the single most compelling, ready-to-use real number in everything found this loop - InfiniteZero Network alone sold $45,108. A story this grounded needs no embellishment.

**Why first:** zero dependencies, zero decisions needed from anyone else, and it's a trust-building piece that helps everything else on this list (a newsletter that can point to $192k in real, transparent fund activity has more credibility asking readers to back a festival crowdfund or a Writer Coin later).

### 2. WaveWarZ weekly recap - do this second

**Effort: medium.** Needs a decision on where it lives (a recurring slot inside @thezao, most likely) and a repeatable weekly habit of reading `wavewarz.info/leaderboards` (no confirmed API yet, so manual for now).

**Impact: medium-high, and recurring.** Real nightly battles, real named winners, real SOL earnings, every single week - this is a genuine content engine, not a one-off. It's the one idea on this list that pays out repeatedly instead of once.

**Why second, not first:** requires setting up a habit rather than writing one piece - more setup cost than the fund deep-dive, but the ongoing payoff is larger.

### 3. ZAOstock countdown - real, but paced by the calendar

**Effort: medium.** Needs a new short-form countdown format (not daily-3, not weekly-recap, not deep-dive - genuinely a fourth shape).

**Impact: high, time-boxed.** The Sept 3 musician-submission deadline is the one truly time-sensitive lever here - pushing that link out matters more the earlier it happens. The Oct 3 event itself is 86 days out, no need to rush tonight, but the countdown format should exist before the next real milestone lands (sound system confirmation, permit approval) so there's somewhere for that update to go.

**Why third:** important, but nothing forces action this week the way the Fund piece or WaveWarZ's nightly cadence does.

### 4. COC Concertz second publication - good idea, blocked on someone else

**Effort: medium-high.** New Paragraph publication, new scoped API key, and - per the original brief - this needs buy-in from whoever runs COC Concertz day-to-day, not just Zaal.

**Impact: medium.** Real content (4 shows, named artists with released work) but low velocity - roughly one show every few months, not a recurring engine like WaveWarZ.

**Why fourth:** the idea doesn't move until that conversation happens. Worth having the conversation, but nothing to build alone right now.

### 5. Post-coined giveaway article - already on its own schedule, high variance

**Effort: low-medium**, but the article itself isn't written yet (planned Jul 14, per doc 958) - the coining decision is trivial once the post exists.

**Impact: high variance.** Modeled on a 1.49M-view reference piece, but that's one data point - could go viral, could not. The per-trade fee only pays out if it actually spikes.

**Why last:** not because it's weak, but because it doesn't need extra prioritization work today - it's already scheduled, and the only real decision (coin it or not) can wait until the draft exists on the 14th.

## The one thing to do this morning

Draft the ZAO Fund deep-dive. Everything needed for it already exists - no new tooling, no one else's buy-in, no calendar dependency. It's the highest-impact, lowest-effort item on the whole list.
