# Two more new Paragraph angles - ZAOstock countdown and ZAO Fund transparency

Date: 2026-07-10 (loop pass 4)
Scope: checked ZAOstock and the ZAO Fund - both real, both currently invisible to newsletter readers.

## What's real, checked today

**ZAOstock - a real festival, 86 days out.** October 3, 2026, 12pm-6pm, Heart of Ellsworth, Ellsworth, Maine. Fiscal sponsorship locked (New Media Commons via Fractured Atlas, plus ENTERACT as production entity). Five named advisors confirmed: Steve Peer, Adam Place, Craig Gonzalez, Tom Fellenz, Tyler Stambaugh. A musician open call is live right now at zaostock.com/musicians/submit with a real Sept 3 deadline. The $10k crowdfund (MaineCF/Fractured Atlas) is already complete, ahead of schedule. Two WaveWarZ battles are planned as part of the day. Source: ZAOOS doc 1007 (readiness audit, validated 2026-07-09), repo `ZAODEVZ/zaostock`.

Two open risk items worth naming honestly, not obstacles to the event being real: sound system booking status is unconfirmed past its own June 1 deadline, and the tent vendor (Wallace Events) agreement is described internally as a "handshake," not signed. This matters for how confidently a countdown series can talk about logistics.

**ZAO Fund - real money moving, zero public storytelling.** A match-funding vehicle on Artizen (artizen.thezao.com), Season 6, curated by Zaal. Real numbers: $10k in fund capital, ~$192k in total artifact sales across 32 accepted projects, with a 1:1 instant match mechanic ($1 matched per $1 sold while the fund lasts). Top sellers: InfiniteZero Network at $45,108, Edge Esmeralda at $30,569. Season 6 runs through July 9, 2026. Source: ZAOOS docs 843 and 947.

The fund's own intake-call notes state plainly: no newsletter or press signal of this activity exists anywhere. $192k in real creator sales is currently invisible to anyone who isn't in the internal loop.

## The new idea, two parts

**Part A - a ZAOstock countdown, a genuinely different format from anything built so far.** Not daily-3 (too small a container for a single festival's arc), not weekly-recap (implies routine, this is a one-time countdown), not deep-dive (too long-form for a quick milestone update). A short countdown post at real milestones as they land - the Sept 3 musician deadline closing, sound system confirmed, permit approved - each grounded in a real number or name, same voice rules as everything else (lowercase, no commas, no bullets). This also gives the newsletter a natural reason to push the Sept 3 open call itself, which is a real, time-sensitive, action-driving link to include.

**Part B - a ZAO Fund deep-dive, lower-effort and ready now.** The `templates/deep-dive.md` format built earlier today fits this directly: one real story, real numbers, real names, no invented framing needed since $192k and 32 real grantees is already more than enough material. This closes the exact gap the fund's own docs flag - zero public visibility into $192k of real activity - and it needs no new mechanic, no new publication, no coordination with anyone outside what Zaal already curates. Of the two, this is the one that's ready to draft immediately.

## What's not resolved here

Whether a ZAOstock-specific fundraising mechanic (e.g. a post-coin tied to the event, similar logic to the giveaway-article idea from an earlier pass) makes sense is a real open question, not decided by this brief - the crowdfund goal is already met, so any additional mechanic would need its own reason to exist beyond the countdown content itself.
